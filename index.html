Our story

Craft made carefully, served simply.

Linden Bakery is imagined as a neighborhood bakery where excellent bread, laminated pastry, and thoughtful coffee feel effortless. The focus is not on excess, but on doing a few things beautifully and consistently every day.

Everything about the space is meant to feel warm and intentional: flour-dusted mornings, good butter, long fermentation, and a room that invites people to stay a little longer than they planned.

A focused menu of breads, pastry, and coffee favorites.
